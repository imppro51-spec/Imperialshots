Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about bookings, videography, delivery & services

How do I book a shoot with Imperial Shots?

You can book directly from our website or after logging into your account. Once logged in, submit your booking details.

Where can I view my bookings?

All your bookings are available on the My Bookings page after login.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes, only Pending bookings can be canceled or rescheduled from the My Bookings section.

What videography services do you provide?

We provide Wedding Films, Pre-Wedding Shoots, Reels & Short Films, and Commercial Videography. Visit the Home Page for full details.

Do you provide cinematic wedding films?

Yes, cinematic storytelling is our specialty. Every wedding film is edited professionally with music and color grading.

How long does it take to deliver photos and videos?

Final delivery usually takes 2–4 weeks, depending on the package and workload.

How will I receive my final photos and videos?

All final content is delivered digitally via Google Drive or private link.

Do you provide raw photos or raw video footage?

Raw footage is available on request and may be chargeable depending on your selected package.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, and cash payments. Full payment details are shared during booking confirmation.

Is advance payment required?

Yes, an advance payment is required to confirm your booking date.

Can I request changes in editing?

Minor revisions are allowed. Major re-edits may involve additional charges.

Do you create Instagram reels and short videos?

Yes, trending reels and short cinematic videos are available as part of selected packages.

Do you take commercial or brand shoots?

Yes, we handle professional ads, brand films, and promotional videos. Contact us via the Contact Page.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, travel and stay costs are discussed separately depending on the location.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us through the Contact Us page or WhatsApp.
