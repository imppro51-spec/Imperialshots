Service Agreements

Welcome to Imperial Shots. This document outlines the terms, conditions and government compliance regulations that govern our videography services.

1. Booking & Payments

  • All bookings must be made via our official checkout page.
  • Advance payment of 20% is required to confirm the booking.
  • Full payment must be received prior to delivery of final video content.
  • Payment methods include online payment gateways as listed on our website.

2. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Cancellations made 7 days before the event will receive a 50% refund of the advance payment.
  • No refund will be issued for cancellations within 7 days of the event.
  • All refunds are processed within 14 working days via the original payment method.

3. Event Guidelines

  • Clients must provide accurate event details including venue, date and timings.
  • Imperial Shots will adhere to all local government regulations and permissions for filming.
  • Any restrictions by event venues must be communicated in advance.

4. Content Delivery

  • Videos are delivered digitally within the agreed timeline.
  • Imperial Shots reserves the right to use portions of the video for promotional purposes unless otherwise agreed.
  • Raw footage is not typically shared unless explicitly requested.

5. Client Responsibilities

  • Ensure safe access to event locations for our team and equipment.
  • Provide any necessary permissions or permits required by local authorities.
  • Communicate any specific requirements or shots prior to the event.

7. Limitation of Liability

  • Imperial Shots is not responsible for any lost or damaged content due to circumstances beyond our control.
  • Clients agree to indemnify and hold harmless Imperial Shots for any claims arising from the event coverage.

8. Government Compliance

  • All videography services comply with the laws and regulations of the Government of India.
  • Any legal obligations regarding drone usage, copyright or public events will be strictly adhered to.
  • Clients must ensure necessary permissions from local authorities for recording at public locations.
